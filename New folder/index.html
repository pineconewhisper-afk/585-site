How it Works

How do I place an order?

Simply add your desired items to your bag using our live menu, enter your delivery coordinates in Monroe County, and choose a dispatch time. Your order is securely formatted and sent to our dispatchers via SMS.

When will I get my ETA?

Order cut-offs are strictly 30 minutes before your selected route begins. You will receive a live ETA via secure text message approximately 30 minutes prior to the start of the route.

Do you sell glass, concentrates, and accessories?

Yes! 585 Direct operates as both a premium mobile dispensary and a full-service smoke shop. Alongside our unmatched variety of premium flower, our live menu constantly rotates with the best concentrates, vapes, edibles, and glass in Rochester.

What is the maximum weight I can order?

Our maximum limit for standard online delivery is 112g (a quarter pound) per order. For Wholesale quantities exceeding 112g, please text 585-363-1284 directly.

Do I need a medical marijuana card?

No! We operate strictly under New York State recreational guidelines. You only need to be 21 years of age or older. We verify your valid Photo ID securely via text message.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept Cash on Delivery, CashApp, Venmo, and Apple Pay. You can select your preferred method securely during checkout.
